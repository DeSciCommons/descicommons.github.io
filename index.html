What is the DeSci community?

The DeSci community is a global community of scientists, advocates and stakeholders that are designing a new way of doing science and a new way of funding science. We are creating a new way of doing science by freeing scientists from overhead and legacy structures and cultures. We are using blockchain and Web3 tools as a root technology for coordination. Bascially, we are aligning the powers of Web3 for the advancement of humanity.

What will be the DeSciCommons?

Today, we have a unique opportunity to rewrite the protocols for how Science and knowledge creation operate. We will co-create DesciCommons guidelines for defining how DeSci projects operate. The best practices and specifications for DeSci will be enforced by DeSci-funding agencies such as Research DAOs. DeSciCommons will allow scientists to gradually move away from traditional science conduct and go full DeSci someday. They allow many science operating systems instead of the current monolithic system. Making it impossible to prevail by just gaming the system so that scientists will start doing really good science again.
